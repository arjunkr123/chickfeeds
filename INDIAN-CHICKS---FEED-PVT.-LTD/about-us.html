About us

 

Established in the year 2010, Cheemafarm poultry and products are manufacturer, supplier, and exporter of poultry products. It is running wonderfully under the valued guidance of an experienced person. In just a small period, we have carved a niche in the Jammu Market. As a company, we are operating from Jammu, Jammu & Kashmir (India) and We are suppling to Punjab, Haryana ,Himachal Pradesh, Delhi and all the other Northern States of India. Harpreet Cheema is the owner of the company.

 

Manufactory

At Cheemafarm & poultry products, we have invested a big amount in developing our services. Supported with technologically upgraded processing, quality testing, packaging, and labeling tools, we try hard to satiate the different needs of the worthy clientele. Additionally, we perform the regular servicing of our machinery for well-organized management of handling the operations. We have specialization over Live Rhode Island Red Chicken, Live RiR Chicken, Live White Leghorn Chicken, Live Kadaknath Chicken, Live Turkey Bird, Live Guinea Fowl Bird, Live Country Chicken, and Live Desi Hen. 

 

Quality Parameters

At Cheemafarm & poultry products, we consider that a completely satisfied customer base is all that a company needs to mark its fame in the business. Considering the things, we have adopted strict quality assessment and acceptable standards for making sure for the ensuring best standards of our offerings. Moreover, we have appointed a break-up team of quality personnel that is hand over to give the quality approved poultry products to our valued clients.
